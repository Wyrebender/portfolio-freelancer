About

First of all, I would like to take a moment to say thank you for spending some time viewing my portfolio website. Hopefully, my portfolio would help you to become more interested into working together as a team.

Having gained a degree in Bachelor of Science and extensive knowledge in web design and development from Full Sail University, I spent over 5 years developing professional web sites and web applications.

Although I am primarily front-end developer, I have utilized skills in Web Design, Server Side Web Development, Web Standards and Usability, Web Production Process, and Mobile Device Development.

I am proficient at user interface design, the core element of website development. I also have effective problem-solving and analytical skills with ability to work independently or in a team environment.

Oh by the way, I personally love to listen to all types of music; especially smooth jazz, electronica and 80s music. One of my favorite artists is Paul Hardcastle (smooth jazz genre). In my downtime, I enjoy road trips, cycling and other outdoor activities.

Project Scenario

A company named Acme Movie Reviews wants to develop a web site that will provide movie review information to their targeted demographic. The site must relay information on the movie along with a review in addition to also incorporating a theater listing that also contains a Google maps API component to let the user see what movies are being shown in their area.

The assignment for this project is to design a user interface for their web site and need me to reflect the desired functionality they want in addition to incorporating aesthetics in to the design in order to further communicate their message to the intended user base. They have no branding and have given me creative freedom in how I approach the look and feel. All they ask if that the UI cater to the demographic Acme Movie Reviews have elaborated on.

Create a design composition for a home page, movie detail page and a cast member page.
